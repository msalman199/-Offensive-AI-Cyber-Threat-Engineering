# Deepfake Social Engineering Awareness

## Attack Vectors
- Impersonation of executives or authority figures
- Fake video calls for authentication bypass
- Fraudulent video testimonials
- Manipulation of public opinion

## Defense Strategies
- Multi-factor authentication beyond video verification
- Out-of-band verification for sensitive requests
- Employee training on deepfake recognition
- Technical detection tools implementation

## Legal and Ethical Considerations
- Unauthorized deepfakes may violate privacy laws
- Potential criminal liability for fraud
- Reputational damage to impersonated individuals
- Need for clear organizational policies
